# Residential GC Workflow + Data Blueprint (Sitesense)
_Generated: 2025-12-19_

This document defines a **residential General Contractor** operating model. It is optimized for remodels, additions, custom homes, and small multi‑family projects, where homeowner communication, selections, allowances, inspections, and change orders dominate success.

## 1) Residential GC Project Lifecycle
**A. Lead Intake → B. Design / Pre‑Con → C. Estimate & Proposal → D. Contracting → E. Permits & Inspections → F. Scheduling & Selections → G. Construction Execution → H. Billing → I. Change Orders → J. Punch & Closeout**

## 2) Core Modules (Residential‑First)
- Lead Intake & Client CRM
- Estimating + Allowances + Selections
- Owner Contract + Trade Agreements
- Permits & Inspections
- Scheduling (trade sequencing)
- Daily Logs + Photos
- Budget Tracking + Progress Billing
- Change Orders
- Punch List & Warranty Closeout

## 3) Required Data Entities
Project, Homeowner, Property, Estimate, EstimateItems, Allowances, Selections, Trades, TradeAgreements, Schedule, Tasks, DailyLogs, Photos, Permits, Inspections, Invoices, Payments, ChangeOrders, PunchItems, Warranties.

## 4) Workflow by Phase
### A) Lead Intake
- Capture homeowner info, address, project type, budget range
- Attach photos, inspiration links, sketches
- Fields: occupied/unoccupied, pets, access constraints

### B) Design / Pre‑Construction
- Site visit and measurements
- Coordinate plans (architect/designer/engineer)
- Early budget check + feasibility

### C) Estimate & Proposal
- Build estimate with clear scope
- Define allowances (fixtures, finishes, appliances)
- Define exclusions clearly
- Output homeowner‑friendly proposal

### D) Contracting
- Sign owner agreement
- Collect deposit
- Execute trade agreements

### E) Permits & Inspections
- Determine permit requirements
- Submit drawings and applications
- Track rough/final inspections

### F) Scheduling & Selections
- Create trade sequence
- Track homeowner selections & deadlines
- Order long‑lead items

### G) Construction Execution
- Daily logs with photos
- Trade coordination
- Quality checks

### H) Billing
- Progress billing or milestone billing
- Track payments received
- Reconcile against budget

### I) Change Orders
- Capture homeowner requests
- Price changes
- Approval before work

### J) Punch & Closeout
- Final walkthrough
- Punch corrections
- Warranty handoff

## 5) Critical Residential Fields
### Project Core
- Address, homeowner contact
- Project type (remodel/addition/new build)
- Budget + contingency

### Allowances & Selections
- Allowance category
- Budgeted amount
- Actual selection + cost
- Over/under tracking

### Permits & Inspections
- Permit type, authority, status
- Inspection dates/results

### Billing
- Invoice #
- Billing milestone
- Amount billed/paid/balance

### Change Orders
- CO #, reason
- Cost/time impact
- Homeowner approval

### Punch & Warranty
- Item, location, trade
- Warranty term
