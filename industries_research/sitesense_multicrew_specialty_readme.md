## Industry Overview
High-volume specialty contractors (roofing, concrete, fencing, hardscape, asphalt) operate on crew efficiency, logistics, and weather-dependent scheduling.
Profitability depends on crew utilization, production rates, and tight material coordination.

## Typical Workflow
- Lead Intake
- Estimate & Scope
- Crew Scheduling
- Material Procurement
- Field Execution
- Quality Check
- Invoice & Closeout

## Key Tasks by Phase
Lead Intake: capture site, access, urgency, weather sensitivity
Estimate: production-based estimating (sq ft, yards, linear feet)
Scheduling: assign internal crews, equipment, trucks
Execution: daily production tracking, weather delays
QC: supervisor inspection
Billing: job-cost vs estimate comparison

## Must-Have Fields
- Crew ID, crew size, skill level
- Production rate per crew
- Equipment assigned
- Material delivery status
- Weather delay tracking
- Cost per job vs estimate

## Critical Screens
- Crew Dispatch Board
- Production Tracker
- Equipment Allocation
- Job Cost Dashboard
- Weather Impact View
